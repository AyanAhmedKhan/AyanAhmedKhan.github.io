Project Overview

This Virtual Lab is part of the Electronic Lab project , created as a part of the academic curriculum at MITS Gwalior. The lab focuses on providing a platform for hands-on virtual experiments in the field of electronics and Internet of things. Through this project, we aim to make theoretical concepts more accessible by allowing students to interact with simulations in real-time.

Our Mentor

The project is guided by our esteemed teacher, Namita Arya Ma'am, who has provided invaluable support and insights. Under her mentorship, we aim to bridge the gap between theory and practice, ensuring that students gain a deeper understanding of core principles through interactive learning.

Our Goal

The goal of this project is to provide an engaging, accessible, and comprehensive learning environment that helps students at MITS Gwalior and beyond, understand and apply the concepts of electronics and circuit theory with ease.
